Tink-her-Hack Hackathon 2024

i was thrilled to be selected for Kerala's largest women-only hackathon, held at Christ College of Engineering on February 24-25, 2024, our team, DevDynamos, embarked on an ambitious project to develop a period tracker for women. Although we didn't complete the project, the experience was incredibly rewarding. We explored new technologies, including MySQL and PostmanAPI, and discovered the value of collaboration, creative problem-solving, and pushing beyond our limits.

Through this journey, we learned to work together seamlessly, think outside the box, and bring ideas to life. Our goal was to create a valuable tool for women's wellness and health tracking, and while we didn't reach the finish line, we're proud of the progress we made. We're already looking forward to the next hackathon, eager to take on new challenges and turn them into opportunities for growth and innovation.
